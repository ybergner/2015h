On the Courseware page you will find the individual and collaborative portions of the math test as well as a demographic survey.

The test questions require numerical responses. Fractions can be written using "/", but no letters are permitted. Once you have decided on an answer, press the "check" button and navigate to the next question.

To answer the questions, you can use the calculator widget that appears at the bottom of the test or your own calculator. You may also want to use paper and a pencil.

Don't spend too long on any one question, and remember that all questions must be answered . If you have to guess, that is OK.

Your participation in this project helps us to improve online math education. Thanks!
